Porrdige

Description

This warm and delicious porridge, made with oats, bananas, and cinnamon, is a perfect breakfast for the winter.

Ingredients

  • 2 and a half cups of water
  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar (optional)
  • 2 bananas - sliced
  • 1 pinch ground of cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup of cold milk (optional)
