Porridge

Description

This warm and delicious porridge, made with oats, bananas, and cinnamon, is a perfect breakfast for the winter.

Ingredients

  • 2 and a half cups of water
  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar (optional)
  • 2 bananas - sliced
  • 1 pinch ground of cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup of cold milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine water and oats in a saucepan. Season to taste with sugar and salt.
  2. Add bananas and cinnamon.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer until the liquid has been absorbed, stirring frequently.
  4. Pour into bowls, and top each with a splash of cold milk.
