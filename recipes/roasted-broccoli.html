Roasted Broccoli

Author: France C | Source: Allrecipes

Description

Roasted broccoli is really easy to make and more flavorful than boiled.

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces of broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Cut the broccoli florets from the stalk. Peel the stalk and slice it into 1/4-inch slices.
  3. Mix the broccoli florets and stem pieces with olive oil in a bowl.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a baking sheet and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Roast the broccoli in the preheated oven until it is tender and lightly browned, about 18 to 20 minutes.
